Before you begin - read carefully

    Time Limit

    You have exactly 30 minutes from the moment you start. The timer begins immediately and cannot be paused. The exam auto-submits when time expires.

    All questions must be attempted. Each question carries equal marks. Unanswered questions score zero — do not leave any blank.

  • 3
    Score Computation

    Your CA score is calculated as (Your Marks ÷ 60) × 25, giving your contribution out of the 25% continuous assessment.

  • 4
    Stay on the Exam Screen

    Do not switch tabs, minimise the browser, or navigate away during the exam. Suspicious activity may flag and invalidate your attempt.

    Your result will be available immediately after submission. Correct answers are not displayed to prevent academic dishonesty.

    Once you click Submit or the timer runs out, the exam is permanently closed for your account. No re-attempts will be granted under any circumstance.

    Stable Internet Required
